Ann Arbor Area Community Foundation

Communication System Design

Overview

Ann Arbor Area Community Foundation (AAACF) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improve the lives of people in Washtenaw County. AAACF is front desk centered office and suffers from low efficiency in customer service. My team and I are to design the reception desk to improve customer service while also considering staff productivity and job satisfaction.

Duration

Dec,2015-Apr,2016

Role

  • UX Designer

Methods

  • Background Research

  • Interview

  • Affinity Diagram

Work Flow

Background Research->Interview Protocol->Affinity Diagram

Background Research

To use industry-quality resources to research organization to gain additional information about the context of project: e.g., organizational structure, role in the community, values, etc.

Interview Protocol

For client-based projects: A client’s existing information workflow; what the workflow is supposed to be, what is or isn’t working, where there are workflow breakdowns, and how people work around them.


Affinity Diagram

Organize a large number of ideas into their natural relationships.
