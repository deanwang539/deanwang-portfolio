Association of Outdoor Recreation Education

Web Usability Evaluation

Project Overview

The Association of Outdoor Recreation and Education (AORE) is an non-profit organization that provides services to professionals and students in the field of outdoor recreation and education. AORE wants to know members thoughts and needs of current membership system in order to improve services.

Duration

Dec,2015-Apr,2016

Role

  • UX Researcher

Methods

  • Interaction Map

  • Interview

  • Personas

  • Scenarios

  • Survey

  • Comparative Analysis

  • Heuristic Evaluation

Work Flow

Interaction Map->Interview->Personas/Scenarios->Comparative Analysis->Survey->Heuristic Evaluation

Interaction Map

Gather screen shots for every action one can take on the websitr, and make a layout that indicates “where you go” when you take each action— what the screen looks like or what happens. Visually indicate subsets of activities in the product, and “jumping points” where a number of actions go to the same place. In this case, we focused on three main features- Membership, News and Give.


Personas

Based on interviews with targer auidence, we constructed different fictitious characters (personas) that represent some of the dimensions of the target population.


Survey

The key purpose of the survey is to better understand the attitudes, behaviors, and characteristics of our target audience, as well as the interactions among different combinations of these.
